Poster Competition“Popularization of Science”

(Open to all science faculty students)

-         To motivate and create interest in school children to do their secondary and university education in science 

-         Will be organized parallel to the Annual Innovation and Invention exhibition, Faculty of Science(Oct 23 -24, 2014) 

-         Main aim is to improve undergraduates’ICT skills and Soft skills 

-         Choose a topic (should be in simple & easy to understand words) in a subject area you like, Convey that knowledge to create an interest in a young mind in an attractive/creative manner using your design skills

-         Poster could be an individual task or a group task 

-         Limit wording to a minimum 

-         Attractive cartoons, pictures, sketches, etc. can be used to a maximum to illustrate your message 

-         Language can be either Sinhala or English 

-         Submit your sketch of the poster in a B4 size MS/WORD file 

-         If the MS/WORD file is more than 2MB, send a PDF file of the whole poster sketch and only the wording at appropriate places in a MS/WORD file 

-         If you make a Sinhala poster, please use “FMBindumathie” Font only 

How to Apply – Email your poster sketchwith your Name/telephone number/student ID number, on or before 25thSept. 2014 (Type  subject as “Poster Competition - applicant’s name”).

Selected posters will be notified on or before 01stOctober for final designing. 

Submission of final version - 10th Oct. 2014
