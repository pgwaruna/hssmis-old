The Annual General Meeting of the Botany Society -2016

The Annual General Meeting of the Botany Society will be Commenced at 2.00 p.m. on 08 wednesday, June 2016 in the Botany Lecture theater the new committee for this year will be accessed in this meeting and furthur the programme of the Botany society for this year is will be discussed. All the students of the Faculty of Science are invited to be in this meeting.
