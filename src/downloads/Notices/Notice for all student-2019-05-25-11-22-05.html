Notice to all Students of the Faculty of Humanities & Social Sciences

All the Students must

  1. produce the identity cards at the main entrances to verify their identity,
  2. always keep the National Identity Card and the University Student Identity Card and produce them when requested by the university Staff members or a member of the Security Service,
  3. assist the security service in verifying the identity of the outsiders when they enter the university premises,
  4. only use the main entrances to enter the university premises,
  5. only bring the essential items into the lecture halls (Students are not allowed to bring their bags into lecture halls, laboratories or any meeting rooms),
  6. report to the security services, if the students get to know about any suspicious persons, packages or incidents in the university premises,
  7. ensure that any kind of unrest does not take place among students or staff on the racial, religious, political or any other bases,
  8. not conduct any kind of unauthorized meetings,
  9. ensure that any kind of unrest does not take place among the students (otherwise immediate action will be taken to suspend the academic activities),
  10. always work towards ensuring the security of the university, and
  11. allow the staff and security personnel to perform all such other acts as may be necessary for ensuring the security of the university.

Dr. Upali Pannilage

Dean, Faculty of Humanities & Social Sciences

University of Ruhuna
